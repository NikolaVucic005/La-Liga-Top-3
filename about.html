About Page

What will i find on this website?

On this website you will find all the important information about La Liga's Top 3 clubs(Real Madrid, FC Barcelona, Atletico Madrid) you will find out about their history, about their number of trophies, about their players and you will even have opportunity to apply for a Job if the position is open.

How to use this website?

The purpose of this website is to grant its Users easy access to all the sections of the website and to make sure you can check out anything that interests you about this website. The way to do that is by the Navigation bar at the top of every page and by arrows, or numbers at the bottom of main pages. The arrows or the numbers at the bottom of each main page will guide you towards, or back to the previous main pages.

How can i apply for a Job?

If there are positions open, on the site of a club, in the Navigation bar you will find the section called 'Job Application'. You will need to fill the form, and then submit it, and after your application review is finished, you will be notified if you are accepted or rejected. Currently only FC Barcelona is looking for employees, but this section is constantly being updated and if any club is looking for employees it will be updated here.
